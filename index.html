Intro

After Haven is a gripping post-apocalyptic adventure set in a world devastated by unknown cataclysms. Players step into the shoes of a resilient survivor navigating through hauntingly beautiful, yet dangerous environments—ranging from crumbling urban ruins to overgrown wilderness. As they explore, players must scavenge for scarce resources, uncover hidden secrets about the downfall of civilization, and confront hostile factions and mutated creatures that threaten their survival. With a rich narrative woven into immersive gameplay, After Haven challenges players to make difficult choices that shape the fate of the remaining survivors and determine the future of this shattered world. Strategic combat, stealth, and exploration blend seamlessly to create an intense, atmospheric experience where every decision counts.

Privacy Policy - After Haven

Effective Date: May 20, 2025

At 80o Studio, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data in After Haven. By playing the game, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Overview

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The game is fully functional without requiring any personal data or online account.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • No names, emails, or contact details
  • No user accounts or passwords
  • No geolocation or IP addresses
  • No payment or billing information
  • No personally linked device IDs

3. In-Game Data Handling

Some non-personal data like game progress or session settings may be stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to us or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

After Haven does not use third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting frameworks. Your data never leaves your device.

5. Data Retention

We do not retain any user data. All in-game data remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the game.

6. Security

While we do not collect personal data, we follow industry-standard practices to protect your device and ensure a secure game experience.

7. Children’s Privacy

After Haven is not intended for children under 13. No personal data is collected, and we comply with child privacy protection laws such as COPPA.

8. Your Rights

Since no data is collected, there is no personal information to access or delete. If this changes in future updates, you will be notified in-game or through our website.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if game systems or features change. All updates will be shared in-game or on this page.
